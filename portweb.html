Coffee Island

A concept that came into my imagination one day while eating a sandwich at Quick Check. As I was eating my lunch, my eyes noticed a wet floor sign labeled "Coffee Island". I imagined an actual island that was made of coffee, then I started sketching some designs for a logo. Once I created the proper logo, I threw together some pictures in Photoshop to create this website banner. Still not sure if Coffee Island should be a store website or a blogging website. Maybe it can be both.
